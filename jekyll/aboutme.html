I started my career at Anafocus, designing tests, and automating measurements for ASIC devices and image sensors characterization.

After the company was acquired by Teledyne, I relocated to their sites in France and China where I focused on camera middleware, system development, and customer support.

Today, as Application Engineer I manage technical support, product development, and client relationships. My role includes coding proof-of-concepts for presales, developing APIs and SDKs, and implementing CI and CD techniques to ensure smooth and reliable integration for our customers. I also train the sales team to help them effectively present our solutions.

I graduated in Telecommunications Engineering at the University of Seville, where I balanced my studies with competitive rowing.

After deciding to focus on my career, I applied to the semiconductor industry filling the space that competitive sport had left in my life and completing my degree.

Later, I completed a Master in Electronics and Embedded Systems at the University of Malaga, which deepened my understanding of embedded systems and low-level programming.

This motivated me into software roles, leading me to a second Master in Cloud Native Applications Development at the University Rey Juan Carlos where I specialized in cloud computing, microservices, and DevOps practices.
